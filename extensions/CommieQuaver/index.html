You have come to download the CommieQuaver Chrome Extension.

CommieQuaver is a Chrome extension which plays communist music. Why, you ask, would you need something like that? You don't. But who cares, it's fun!

Updates will be released frequently; check this site frequently to stay updated.



As the author refuses to cough up $5 to publish to the Chrome Store for now, you'll have to follow these steps to add CommieQuaver to Chrome.


Step I
Download the zip file for the extension here, and extract it anywhere.
Step II
Go to chrome://extensions and select 'developer mode' in the top-right corner.

Step III
Select 'Load unpacked' in the top-left corner and open the folder you extracted the zip file to.

Step IV
Select the puzzle icon next to the search bar in Chrome. A drpdown will show all your extensions, including CommieQuaver. You may pin it if you want.


You have now downloaded CommieQuaver. Try your best to enjoy!
